    Definitions

    • Logo tree.
      This results shows the hierarchical tree with its logo alignment of one cluster.
      The logos are shown in Forward (left logo) and Reverse (right logo) orientation.
    • Branch-motifs.
      On the trees are displayed the branch number that is the number in which the motifs were incorporated in the tree.
      The Branch-Motifs table shows the logo in both orientations and the link to the file in TRANSFAC format with the branch-motif.
